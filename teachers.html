What is Teachers?

Teachers is an irreverent comedy about six elementary school teachers whose personal lives bleed into their professional lives.

The show stars The Katydids, who also created, executive produced, and wrote the entire season. Season one consists of ten episodes and includes an abundance of talented guest stars, including Rob Corddry, Rob Riggle, Lacey Chabert, and Alison Brie, who is also an executive producer on the show. Key and Peele’s Emmy-nominated Ian Roberts and Jay Martel showrun. Teachers is based on the same-titled web series released in 2012, which was directed by Matthew Miller and produced by Cap Gun Collective.

Where Can I See Teachers?

The series debuts on TV Land on Wednesday, January 13, 2016, at 11:00PM following the one-hour season premiere of Younger. It will run every Wednesday at 10:30PM thenceforth.

Don’t have cable? No problem! Teachers will be streaming on Hulu, and will be available to download on iTunes, Amazon, Google Play, Xbox, PlayStation, and Verizon.

You can also watch the Teachers pilot for free on the TV Land website!
